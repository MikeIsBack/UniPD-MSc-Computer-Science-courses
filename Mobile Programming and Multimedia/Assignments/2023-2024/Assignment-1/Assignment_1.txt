Exercise on cross-platform development frameworks

Choose a framework for cross-platform development that was not discussed during the lessons  (Cordova, Solar 2D, Flutter, ReactNative) and classify it according to Raj and Tolety classification. Prepare a presentation of 5 minutes (max 5 slides) which briefly introduces :

the chosen framework, 
why you choose it;
its classification.
The presentation must contain references to the used documentation.

This exercise must be done in groups of two students and will be presented on the 19th March 2024.

The presentation must be uploaded before 15th March 2024 1.00 p.m.
